Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Karunarasa Stotram

Shrimatpayonidhiniketanachakrapan Bhogindrabhogamanirajitapunyamurte
(Pathabheda Bhogindrabhogamaniranjita Punyamurte)

Meaning: O Lord Narasimha, dwelling in the ocean of milk and holding the divine discus, radiant with purity — You are the source of all goodness and mercy.
Significance: Invoking the Lord’s serene yet powerful form inspires purity and inner peace in devotees.

Yogisha Shashvata Sharanya Bhavabdhipota Lakshminrusimha Mama Dehi Karavalambam

Meaning: O Eternal Lord of Yogis, Savior of those lost in worldly existence — extend Your protective hand to me.
Significance: Symbolizes surrender — seeking divine help to cross the ocean of life’s struggles.

Samsaradavadahanakarabhikaroru Jvalavalibhiratidagdhatanuruhasya
Tvatpadapadmasarasiruhamagatasya

Meaning: O compassionate Lord, whose hands burn away worldly pain — I have taken refuge at Your lotus feet.
Significance: Teaches that sincere devotion purifies the heart and brings peace amidst life’s fire.

॥ Iti Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Karavalamba Stotram Samaptam ॥
