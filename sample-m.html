श्री लक्ष्मी नरसिंह करावलम्ब स्तोत्रम्

By Adi Shankaracharya | A Prayer for Divine Protection and Grace

Shrimatpayonidhiniketanachakrapan Bhogindrabhogamanirajitapunyamurte
(Pathabheda Bhogindrabhogamaniranjita Punyamurte)

Explanation

Lord Krishna advises Arjuna to focus on action rather than the outcome. One should perform duties sincerely, with devotion and selflessness, leaving the results to the Divine. This verse encapsulates the spirit of Karma Yoga — doing your duty without attachment.

✨ "Act with purpose, not with expectation." – Inspired by the Bhagavad Gita
