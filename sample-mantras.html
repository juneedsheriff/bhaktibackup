Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Karunarasa Stotram

Shrimatpayonidhiniketanachakrapan Bhogindrabhogamanirajitapunyamurte
(Pathabheda Bhogindrabhogamaniranjita Punyamurte)

Meaning: O Lord Narasimha, who dwells in the ocean of milk and wields the sacred discus! You shine in divine radiance, adored by Adisesha, symbolizing eternal wisdom and purity.
Significance: This verse invokes calmness and purity, dispelling inner turbulence. It’s believed to bring clarity and peace of mind through divine protection.

Yogisha Shashvata Sharanya Bhavabdhipota Lakshminrusimha Mama Dehi Karavalambam

Meaning: O protector of yogis, eternal refuge of all beings! You are the divine boat that helps cross the ocean of worldly life. Extend Your compassionate hand to save me.
Significance: This sloka teaches surrender and trust. Chanting it strengthens faith and helps devotees overcome obstacles through divine grace.

Samsaradavadahanakarabhikaroru Jvalavalibhiratidagdhatanuruhasya
Tvatpadapadmasarasiruhamagatasya

Meaning: O blazing one! Your divine arms burn away the pains of worldly fire. I surrender at Your lotus feet seeking peace and liberation.
Significance: Symbolizing purification, this verse reminds that divine power destroys suffering, paving the way for inner enlightenment.

॥ Iti Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Karavalamba Stotram Samaptam ॥
